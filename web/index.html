ABOUT US

At Mind Over Matter, we aim to promote positive mental health and self care practices for all our clients and followers. We strive to bring awareness to mental health disorders and erase the stigma surrounding them by supporting people who face these challenges on a daily basis. We do this by encouraging conversation on the topic and proactive measures to help navigate the day to day struggles of suffering with mental health ilnesses.

Mind Over Matter was founded in 2021 and has been supported through government grants since our inception. We are a not for profit organisation and are well reknowned within the sector in our short lifetime.
